Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Masonry Gallery Maker ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the App.

Information We Collect

Masonry Gallery Maker does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

The App does not require user registration or account creation.

Images and Uploaded Content

Images uploaded to the App are processed locally within the Canva app environment for the sole purpose of generating masonry-style image layouts.

The App does not:

  • Store uploaded images on external servers
  • Transmit uploaded images to third parties
  • Share uploaded images with other users

Uploaded content remains under the user's control.

Analytics and Tracking

Masonry Gallery Maker does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, cookies, or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

The App operates within the Canva platform and may rely on Canva-provided functionality to deliver its features.

Please refer to Canva's own privacy policies for information regarding Canva's handling of data.

Data Security

Because uploaded images are processed locally and are not stored on external servers operated by the App developer, the risk of unauthorized access through the App is minimized.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
